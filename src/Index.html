About Us

GHESCB has been in operation since 2010, working in the UK, and has partnerships with locally based African CBO in Kenya, Malawi, Cameroon, Zimbabwe, Nigeria, Angola, Uganda, Namibia, and Zambia

We aim to help improve lives by improving how you work by developing capacity of the public, private and civil societies, NGOs, churches and individuals through Training, system strengthening and Consultancy worldwide. 

To help improve lives of the individuals, communities and safeguarding the vulnerable members in the society through providing knowledge, competences and skills to professionals and non-professionals to deliver high quality services in health, education and research and help improve and save lives, reduce poverty, strengthen health systems, increase girls’ access to education and improve health outcomes.

Why Us

We are engaged in different training and technical advisor on variety of health, education and nutrition programs and support of various programs and organisations worldwide. Here are some of our recent activities in Africa, Asia and Europe. We are strong advocates for Girls education and reducing teenager pregnancies. We support programs in the last mile communities and are devoted to improving the health and development outcomes of the disadvantaged in the communities..

Dr Fletcher M Phiri

Our Partners

Kenya

ACHESREM

Africa Centre for Health Systems Research & Management (ACHESREM) is a Non-Governmental Organization registered in Kenya with the aim of strengthening evidence-based programming and policy processes for expansion of equity in access to livelihoods and for attainment of quality, healthy lives for the people of Kenya

Uganda

AUPWAE-ASSOCIATION

AUPWAE was founded in 1992 and registered as a non-profit making organization in 1998 with a mandate to operate in all districts Uganda. AUPWAE boasts of a membership of 300 professional women working in both private and public sectors in all regions of the country.

Uganda

BIDS

Building-community Initiatives for Development & Self-reliance (BIDS). Foundation was established and registered in Soroti District in 2012 as a Community Based Organization. BIDS Foundation is founded on the principle of Building on the Initiatives of the communities to ensure Development and Self-reliance among them through capacity strengthening.

Malawi

Kapiri Communinty Development Organization

KACODO’s vision is healthy and literate community which is self-reliant. Its mission is to facilitate development interventions in education, health and environmental management. KACODO believes in strengthening solidarity with organisations and institutions at community level including girls’ initiatives in schools, mother groups, reflect circles, CBOs, School Management Committees, PTA, teachers’ organisations and linking them across national regional and international levels.

Namibia

Family Aids Caring Trust (FACT)

Is a registered NGO situated in Tsumeb town in the Oshikoto region. FACT strives to reduce the prevalence of HIV/AIDS in Namibia and mitigate the impact of infection/ illness and deaths on individuals’ families and communities through strengthening the ability of local community groups to cope up with the AIDS epidemic.

Support Us

Get Back to School

Help us work in communities to support return to school initiative for girls in rural areas. Many girls in developing countries are not in school for numerous reasons. Many drop-out due to early pregnancy as young as 13 years. They are left without hope and living in poverty with all future dreams lost.

Support Us

Teenager pregnancy

Working in partnership with Kapiri Community Development Organisation (KACODO), we help support young girls who dropped out of school get back to school through parents, teachers, religious leaders, teenagers and community engagement and education in the rural communities of Malawi. we are working with communities to fight existing out of wedlock pregnancy stigma and support social/cultural and behaviour change. sponsor a girl.
